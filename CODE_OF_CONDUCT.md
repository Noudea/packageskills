# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want `packageskills` to be a welcoming, respectful project for everyone.

Examples of expected behavior:

- be respectful and constructive
- assume good intent and communicate clearly
- focus feedback on the work, not the person
- help keep discussions technical, useful, and inclusive

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- personal attacks or insulting language
- publishing private information without permission
- repeated disruptive or bad-faith behavior

## Scope

This applies to project spaces and project-related interactions, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and review comments.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation for behavior that violates this code.

If you need to report a problem, contact the maintainers through a private contact method listed on GitHub rather than filing a public issue.
